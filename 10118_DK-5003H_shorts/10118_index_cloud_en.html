Care & Maintenance
For Enduring, Unchanged Condition byelse Premium Care Guide
Cold Water Separate Wash

We recommend washing separately in cold or lukewarm water below 30°C with a neutral detergent.

No Tumble Drying

High-temperature tumble drying can cause fabric shrinkage and functional damage, so please avoid it.

Use Neutral Detergent

Fabric softeners, bleaches, and strong enzyme detergents can damage functional fibers.

Shade Dry Naturally

To maximize longevity, natural drying in a well-ventilated shade, away from direct sunlight, is recommended.
