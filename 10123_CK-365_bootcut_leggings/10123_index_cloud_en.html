Care & Info
For unchanging condition for a long time byelse Premium Care Guide
Cold Wash

We recommend separate washing with neutral detergent in cold or lukewarm water below 30 degrees.

No Tumble Dry

Please avoid using a high-temperature dryer as it causes fabric shrinkage and functional damage.

Neutral Detergent

Fabric softeners, bleach, and strong enzyme detergents can damage functional yarns.

Dry in Shade

You can wear it longest if you dry it naturally in a well-ventilated shade away from direct sunlight.
